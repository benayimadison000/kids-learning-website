1. The Red Cat

The cat is red.

It sat on the mat.

The cat can run fast.

It has a big hat.

The hat is black.

The cat likes to nap.

It naps in the sun.

The sun is hot.

The cat is happy.

It purrs all day.

2. My Big Dog

I have a big dog.

His name is Max.

Max likes to dig.

He digs in the mud.

The mud is wet.

Max runs to me.

He shakes his fur.

Water goes splash!

I hug my dog.

Max is my best pal.

3. The Little Bird

A little bird sits on a twig.

The bird is blue.

It sings a song.

The song is sweet.

The bird sees a worm.

It hops down quick.

The bird gets the worm.

It flies up high.

The sky is bright.

The bird is glad.

4. Fun at the Park

We go to the park.

The park has swings.

I swing high up.

My sister slides down.

The slide is fast.

We run on the grass.

The grass is green.

We chase a ball.

The ball is red.

We play all day.

5. The Brave Frog

A frog lives by the pond.

The frog is green.

It jumps on a log.

The log is long.

A fish swims by.

The frog says hop!

It catches a bug.

The bug is small.

The frog smiles big.

It is brave and strong.

6. A Sunny Day

The sun shines bright.

It is a sunny day.

I ride my bike.

The bike is blue.

I ride to the lake.

The lake has ducks.

The ducks quack loud.

I feed them bread.

They like the crumbs.

I smile and wave.

7. The Lost Kite

My kite is red and white.

It flies in the wind.

The wind blows hard.

The kite goes up high.

Oh no, it gets stuck!

It is in a tree.

I climb the tree.

I get my kite.

Now it flies again.

I am so happy.

8. The Shiny Shell

We walk on the beach.

The sand is warm.

I find a shell.

The shell is shiny.

It has pink stripes.

I hold it to my ear.

I hear the waves.

The waves crash loud.

I keep the shell.

It is my treasure.

9. Sam and the Jam

Sam likes jam on toast.

The jam is sweet.

He spreads it thick.

Sam eats his toast.

It tastes so good.

He licks his lips.

Mom says clean up.

Sam wipes his chin.

He helps in the kitchen.

Sam is a good boy.

10. The Magic Seed

I plant a small seed.

The seed is brown.

I give it water.

The sun helps it grow.

A green sprout comes up.

It grows tall fast.

Now it has flowers.

The flowers are yellow.

Bees come to visit.

The seed made magic!
