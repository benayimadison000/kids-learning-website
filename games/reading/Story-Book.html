Mila Finds a Tiny Seed

Story 1 of 20

About 15 sentences · Grade 2 reading

1. Mila found a tiny brown seed resting quietly under a big shady tree today.

2. She held the seed gently and wondered what kind of plant it might become.

3. Her teacher said every seed carries a secret waiting patiently for the right moment.

4. Mila placed the seed carefully inside a small pot filled with soft dark soil.

5. She pressed the soil lightly so the seed felt safe, warm, and tucked in.

6. Mila added a little water, watching it soak slowly into the thirsty ground.

7. She placed the pot near the window where bright sunlight shone through each morning.

8. Every day, Mila checked the pot and whispered encouraging words to the hidden seed.

9. She wondered if the seed could somehow hear her gentle voice and friendly hopes.

10. One morning, Mila noticed a tiny green sprout pushing bravely through the soft soil.

11. She felt proud because her careful work helped the seed begin its growing journey.

12. The sprout stretched toward the warm sunlight like a dancer reaching for the sky.

13. Mila promised to keep watering it gently so it stayed strong, healthy, and growing.

14. She imagined the sprout becoming a tall plant with bright leaves waving happily.

15. Mila learned that small actions, done patiently, can help something wonderful slowly grow.

Think and talk

  • What helped the tiny seed begin growing into a sprout?
  • Why do you think Mila felt proud when she saw the sprout?
